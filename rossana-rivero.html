The Foundation

The Architect
of Free Will

Origin

I was born in Peru and raised in Uruguay. The surrounding cultural map never made sense to me — people seemed to blindly follow guidelines they did not understand nor question. At a young age, I decided my life was mine to lead. If I was to fail, I would do so grandly and by my own design, but never as a result of giving away my free will.

The Pursuit of Meaning

My life has since been a continuous pursuit of meaning across vast emotional landscapes. Change has been my constant companion; intuition, my inner compass. I am a seeker of personal truth, an author in the making with an undying thirst for transmutation.

The Integrated Map

My practice, Changeology, is the manifestation of my life's journey. It is where my professional background — a mixture of social sciences, organizational communications, integrative nutrition, and energy medicine — meets the sacred. I have combined these paths with my entrepreneurial skills into an experiential methodology that integrates the Mind, the Body, and the Spirit.

The Invitation

The wounds that once defined me are now the gifts I share. In my presence, you will receive highly personalized guidance to navigate the unknown with curiosity rather than fear. I provide the grounded space where your intuition becomes your map, allowing you to inhabit your new territory with clarity. Mine is a story of empowerment — I invite you to write yours as well.
